Privacy Policy — TarotMe

Last updated: 09.12.25

1. Introduction

TarotMe is designed to provide tarot readings without collecting personal information. We value your privacy and intentionally avoid storing any user data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

TarotMe does not collect or store:

  • Personal details (name, email, age, etc.)
  • Account or login information
  • Device identifiers
  • Tarot reading history
  • Saved cards or logs
  • Analytics or user tracking data

All readings are generated temporarily and disappear when the session ends.

3. Tarot Readings and AI

When a tarot reading is generated, card information is processed by an AI model (such as Llama via Ollama) running locally on your device.

No reading data is uploaded or stored.

4. Cookies and Tracking

TarotMe does not use cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data, including information from children. All use is anonymous.

6. Changes to This Policy

If any future version introduces optional features that require storing user data, users will be informed and asked for consent.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
